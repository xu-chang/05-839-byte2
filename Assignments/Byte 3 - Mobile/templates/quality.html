Quality: About This Data Set

Data used in the visualization comes from the data collected on my personal mobile phone by app AWARE from around 7pm on Oct 13th to 8pm on Oct 17th.

Is the data complete?

The AWARE app has never crashed. I always ensures WiFi connection for it to upload data promptly. MySQL database on the cloud has been working without issue. Manual inspection of the tables and the resulting visualization shows that the data makes sense and are continuous, that is, no significant gap in time that lacks records. Therefore, I would consider this data complete.

Is the data coherent?

Yes, after inspection, all fields are of correct type. There is no column shift, no typos, battery status are within 0-100, light intensities are larger or equal to 0. Timestamps are correctly recorded and can be interpreted by Tableau without error.

Is the data correct?

I found that luminance record sometimes reaches more than 20000, which seems unlikely since the majority of records are within 0~1000 range. I think it is the problem with the sensor, so I removed records with larger than 1000 luminance with Tableau's filtering function. On the otherhand, the curve of the remaining battery life is continuous, no sudden changes, are within the expected range and matches my memory of when I charged the phone and when I unplugged the cord.
