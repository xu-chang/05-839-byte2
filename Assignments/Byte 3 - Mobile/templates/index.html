Relationship Between Battery Status and Ambient Light

As a background story, my mobile phone charger is at my Nightstand, far from my work desk, therefore I only charge my phone when I'm on bed, lights off and ready to sleep.

From the figure we can see that I went to sleep at 4 or 5 am each day, except on Oct 15 I went to bed early in the evening since I have a morning class (but still got up in the night for two hours to play with my phone...). I can also see that I sleep for a stable 8 hours a day (count from the bottom of the battery status curve which is when I start to charge the phone to the point where the remaining battery starts to descrease). Combining with the light statistics, we can clearly see that the light luminance goes to zero when the phone starts charging, suggesting the light in the room is turned off. On Oct 17, the light level gradually goes up before I woke up, that is because my room has a east-facing window and Oct 17 is the only day when the sun is up in the morning, previously we had overcasts.
